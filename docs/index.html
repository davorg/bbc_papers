British Newspaper Front Pages From the BBC

Every morning on the BBC News web site, a new story is published which includes images of the front pages of the British newspapers. I find this an invaluable resource as it allows me to get an overview of what the British press is saying without having to actually buy any of the papers. Or, even, visit a newsagent.

The only problem with this situation is that the story tends to vanish from the front page before lunchtime and it's hard to find versions from previous days.

This site attempts to solve that problem by scraping data from the BBC site every day in order to keep a history of all of these pages.
